Frequently Asked Questions

Who should I contact about my schedule? Your guidance counselor.
Who is my guidance counselor? Students are required to attend school. If they are late to school, students are required to go to the main office to get a late pass before attending class. Late to school is defined as after 8:15. Students cannot be picked up early after 2:00 to prevent traffick. Juniors and seniors with a driver's license are allowed to drive to and park at school, provided they buy a parking pass. CLubs occur during advisory or after school, and the school opens at 7AM and closes at 6PM.
